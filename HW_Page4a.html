Humidity

The humidity at a location does not seem to be following any clear relationship with its latitude. But as seen in the plot, the places near the equator, between 20 degrees above and below the equator, we have very highly humid regions. There are regions of low humidity near 20 degrees, and again the humidity rises as we move towards poles.
